Dear Mother

I Remember

my first memory of this world

pain and suffering in your eyes

your cries of madness

when you tried to strangle me

asking me why i would not die


how you starved me for days

locked me in bathroom

how desperatly i wanted you to hold me


how you kicked me down the stairs

i was bleeding and crying

how desperatly i wanted you to pick me


how you beat me in your madness

asking me why i would not die

how you found pleasure in pressing my broken bones

how desperatly i wanted you to kiss me


I wish i could tell you


how cruel the world have been

how easily i trusted them

yet they always hurt me


how scared i am of this world

all i see is people who want to kill me

always planning to hurt me


how mad i am at this world

i wish i could explode

i wish i could destroy everything


how i love this world

how mad i am in love with music

how mad i am in love with colors

how i love to dance with them


how alone i am in this world

yet there are worlds inside me

some are cruel

some are beautiful

a heaven

where people are kind

a mother who loves me


how sad i am in this world

how you poisoned me before i was born

yet i would not die

how i wish i did not exist and you were happy

how i wish i could dissolve into darkness

take away your pain

i am sorry i caused you so much suffering

i am sorry i did not die
